Vitalogy

Pearl Jam

Release Date 1994

This album is what really got me into Pearl Jam. It has some great songs such as Betterman, Nothingman, Corduroy and Immortality.
The album is a more mellow than their first album so it is a great ablum to listen to if you just want to sit back and relax.

Band Members and Producer

  • Mike McCready - Lead Guitar
  • Stone Gossard - Guitar
  • Jeff Ament - Bass
  • Matt Cameron - Drums
  • Brendan O'Brien - Producer
